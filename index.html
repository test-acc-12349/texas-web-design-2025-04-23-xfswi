Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in your web design packages?

Our comprehensive packages include custom design, responsive development, SEO optimization, SSL security, premium hosting, daily backups, and ongoing support. All projects come with unlimited revisions until you're 100% satisfied.

How long does the website development process take?

Most projects are completed within 2-4 weeks. Simple sites can be done in as little as 1 week, while more complex projects may take 6-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What ongoing support do you provide?

We offer 24/7 monitoring, regular updates, security patches, and technical support. Our maintenance plans include content updates, performance optimization, and monthly analytics reports.

How do you handle website security?

Security is our top priority. We implement SSL certificates, regular malware scans, firewall protection, and automated backups. All sites are hosted on enterprise-grade servers with 99.9% uptime guarantee.

What are your payment terms?

We offer flexible payment plans with a 50% deposit to start and the remaining balance due upon completion. Monthly payment options are available for larger projects. We accept all major credit cards and ACH transfers.
